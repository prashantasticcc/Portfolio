Modular & sustainable websites that you own

Websites can make or break a business. After years of deep experience in both web design and Webflow development I know how to build a website that you will be able to manage and your customers will enjoy using.

Design

I design websites that follow both visual designs and usability principles practised over years of working as a UI/UX Designer. Before pushing any pixel though, we carefully discuss and identify strategic needs for your specific business to make sure we get the big picture right.

Development

I offer pixel-perfect, sustainable, SEO-optimised Webflow development. Every project is built using proven best practices yet without a dev jargon that makes you want to cry. I can build complex CMS structures and advanced animations if your business needs those.

Maintenance

Careful! There’s a high chance you won’t need maintenance services. I provide a training on how to add and update content for every project I’m taking. However, if you need long-term support, have an advanced request or simply want to save a bit of time - I can help.

Consultations

I offer case by case consultations and Webflow trainings for marketers, designers, developers or anybody who wants to upskill their Webflow game. If you have any individual consultancy needs please reach out at bencelinskidigital@gmail.com
