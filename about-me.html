→ Currently, at the University of Toronto, I am specializing in User Experience Design and Information Systems and Design. Through my architectural learnings, I have worked towards creating strategies and experiences for invisible layers of society and culture.

✹ I love working on projects that have playful and interactive approach to tackling hard and important social topics such as the lack of educational systems and enhancing accessibility in workspaces. ​

2023 • Ontario Digital Service Co-op

Check out Ontario Digital Service
Thrilled to be a part of the Experience Design Lab at ODS, where I'm crafting accessible and inclusive digital experiences for Ontarians. Check out Microsoft dynamics 365 customer service

2022 • Conference Director and President

Check out Master of Information Student Council
I was responsible for overseeing all aspects of the Masters of Information Council, leading its strategy and goals. I served as the Co-director of iConference 2023, leading the MISC Executive team in the facilitation of research and development working groups that focus on accessibility, diversity, and inclusivity.

2021 • Strategic Designer

Check out Archohm Consults
Formerly, I worked towards designing a matrixthat aims to understand intangible cultural heritage and its role in urban rejuvenation. I also facilitated the development of the tourism redevelopment plan for the biggest temple city in India - Bhubneshwar and the state with the richest biodiversity in India - Meghalaya.

2021 • Researcher and Thesis Mentor

Check out The Design Village
In collaboration with The Design Village, I worked towards enhancing my teaching and research skills by facilitating the development of various research collaterals. I guided various final-year research projects on Accessible design and Sustainable Fashion. I was also a mentor for MUJI +TDV Green Carpet, raising awareness about sustainable fashion.

2021 • Award winning Research Thesis

Check out My Thesis
Previously, I was researching how the integration of Interactive components in architectural spaces offers the potential to enhance the experience of built environments. My research won the Best Research Project at University Level and GICEA Gold Medal for Best Thesis at the state level.
